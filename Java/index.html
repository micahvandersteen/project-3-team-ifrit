About

Team Ifrit proudly presents our final project for our Data Visualization and Analytics Bootcamp Cohort 6. Over the last 6 months we have put learned a lot and now take several elements of what we have learned and put them together in this presentation. We began with the problem of building a model that could predict the rise and fall of the stock market based on news headlines. For example, do stock prices rise with more positive news? Do certain kinds of events effect stock prices? Are certain NLP libraries better to use when finding these relationships?
